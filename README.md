📖 # Project Overview
A dynamic tool visualizing $9.8M in sales via Pivot Tables and Slicers. It tracks KPIs like profit, quantity, and average discounts while providing a geographical customer breakdown. By identifying top and bottom revenue-generating products, it offers a clear summary of overall business performance.


🚀KPIs:
* 💰 Total Sales: $9.89M
* 📈 Total Profit: $1.43M
* 📦 Total Quantity: 37,873 Units
* 📉 Avg. Discount: 16%
* 🎯 Margin %: 14%
  

🗺️ Geographic Insights:
* Customer Map: Visualized customer density across the US.
* Top Hubs: California & New York identified as the highest-performing markets.


📦 Category & Sub-Category Analysis:
* Profit Leader: Technology accounts for 50% of total profit.
* Volume Leader: Office Supplies drive the highest quantity of sales (61%).
* Trend Tracking: A combined Line & Bar chart tracks the relationship between Sales and Profit for every sub-category, identifying where we are selling high but profiting low.


🏆 Product Performance:
* The Stars: Identified top revenue generators like Canon Copiers and Samsung Galaxy phones.
* The Underperformers: Highlighted lowest revenue products to suggest inventory pivots.

  
⚡ Interactive Functionality:
* Dynamic Slicing: Users can filter the entire report by Year, Quarter, Category, and Region to find specific insights in seconds.


🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* Tool: Microsoft Excel (Advanced).
* Data Modeling: Pivot Tables & Pivot Reporting.
* Visualizations: Pivot Charts, Choropleth Maps, and Slicers for interactivity.
* UI/UX: Custom Dashboard Design (Grid alignment, icon integration, and color theory).


🏗️Development Process:
* Data Cleaning: Used Excel to handle missing values and format currency/dates for consistency.
* Analysis: Created Pivot Tables to summarize millions of rows into the categories you see (Category, Region, State).
* Interactivity: Connected Slicers to multiple Pivot Charts using "Report Connections" so the whole dashboard updates with one click.
* Design: Formatted the layout with a dark-mode theme and custom shapes to create a modern, app-like user interface.


💡 Key Business Insights
* Profit vs. Volume: While Office Supplies sell the most units, Technology is the primary profit engine. High-volume, low-margin items (like Tables) may need a pricing strategy review.
* Regional Focus: The West and East regions are the most mature markets. Expansion efforts should focus on scaling the South and Central regions.
* Discount Impact: With an average discount of 16%, further analysis is recommended to see if aggressive discounting is hurting the overall 14% profit margin.
